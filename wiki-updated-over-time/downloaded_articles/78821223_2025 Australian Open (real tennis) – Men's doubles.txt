The '''2025 [[Real Tennis]] Australian Open''' will be the 93rd edition of the [[Australian Open (real tennis)|Australian Open]]. It will be held at the [[Hobart Real Tennis Club]] in [[Hobart]], [[Tasmania]] between January 6â€“14, 2025 and will be organised by the [[Australian Real Tennis Association]]. The men's event will be the first event in the qualifying series for the 2027 Real Tennis World Championship.<ref>{{cite web |title=Australian Open - Draw and Results for 2025 |url=https://www.hobarttennis.com.au/draws |publisher=Hobart Real Tennis Club |access-date=31 December 2024}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Australian Open 2025 |date=31 December 2024 |url=https://www.irtpa.com/ladder/434/ |publisher=International Real Tennis Professionals Association |access-date=22 May 2024}}</ref> The event will be held alongside festivities for the 150th anniversary of the founding of the Hobart club. The men's draw will be the first [[Grand Slam (real tennis)|grand slam]] event of the year.<ref>{{cite web|title = Australian Open & Australian Women's Open Singles & Doubles |url=https://rmtc.com.au/events/australian-open-mens-womens-singles-doubles-2 |publisher=Royal Melbourne Tennis Club |access-date=31 December 2024}}</ref>

The men's singles draw will feature the return of current [[Real Tennis World Championship|World Champion]] [[Camden Riviere]] after a five-year absence at the event. Defending men's champion [[John Lumley (real tennis)|John Lumley]] will not return to defend his title. World Number 3 [[Nick Howell (real tennis)|Nick Howell]] was originally due to play, but withdrew prior to the start of the tournament. The women's singles draw will be defended by incumbent World Champion [[Claire Fahey]].
