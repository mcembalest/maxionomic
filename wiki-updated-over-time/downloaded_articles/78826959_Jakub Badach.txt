'''Kuba Badach''' (<small>Polish pronunciation</small>: {{IPAc-pl|'|k|u|b|a|-|'|b|a|d|a|x}}; born 24 August 1976)<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.filmweb.pl/person/Jakub+Badach-812141|title=Jakub Badach|website=Filmweb}}</ref><ref name="auto5">{{Cite web |title=Kuba Badach &#124; bilety na wydarzenia 2023/2024 |url=https://www.ebilet.pl/artysta/kuba-badach |website=eBilet |language=Pl}}</ref><ref name="auto1">{{Cite web |title=Kuba Badach: urodziny, rodzina, kariera |url=https://party.pl/ludzie/kuba-badach-29906-r7/ |website=Party |language=Pl}}</ref> is a Polish singer, composer, arranger, music producer, [[music journalist]] and television personality. He's a co-founder and member of the band [[Poluzjanci]] and member of the band [[The Globetrotters]].<ref name="auto" /><ref name="auto5" /><ref name="auto1"/> He's been nominated for the two [[Fryderyk]] awards.<ref>{{Cite web |date=May 10, 2011 |title=FRYDERYK 2011 – nominowani i laureaci |url=https://fryderykfestiwal.pl/fryderyk-2011/ |access-date=2024-01-01 |website=Fryderyk Festiwal |language=Pl}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=April 27, 2012 |title=FRYDERYK 2012 – nominowani i laureaci |url=https://fryderykfestiwal.pl/fryderyk-2012/ |access-date=2024-01-01 |website=Fryderyk Festiwal |language=PL}}</ref>
 
In 2024, Badach became a coach on [[The Voice of Poland season 15|the fifteenth season]] of ''[[The Voice of Poland]]''. Badach was the winning coach when his artist Anna Iwanek won the seesson.
