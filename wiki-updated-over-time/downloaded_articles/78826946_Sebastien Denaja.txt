'''Sébastien Denaja''' (born 14 January 1979) is a French politician serving as a member of the [[Regional Council of Occitania]] since 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.midilibre.fr/2021/06/28/sete-sebastien-denaja-veut-sinvestir-sur-les-thematiques-territoriales-comme-regionales-9636894.php|title=Sète : Sébastien Denaja veut s'investir sur les thématiques territoriales comme régionales|work=[[Midi Libre]]|date=29 June 2021|language=fr}}</ref> From 2012 to 2017, he was a member of the [[National Assembly (France)|National Assembly]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.midilibre.fr/2022/06/20/legislatives-la-reaction-de-lancien-depute-ps-de-la7e-sebastien-denaja-a-lelection-du-candidat-rn-10384598.php|title=Législatives : la réaction de l'ancien député PS de la 7e, Sébastien Denaja, à l'élection du candidat RN|work=[[Midi Libre]]|date=21 June 2022|language=fr}}</ref>
