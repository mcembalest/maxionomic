{{1989 Ohio Valley Conference football standings}}
The '''1989 Morehead State Eagles football team''' represented [[Morehead State University]] as a member of the [[Ohio Valley Conference]] (OVC) during the [[1989 NCAA Division I-AA football season]]. Led by sixth-year head coach [[Bill Baldridge]], the Eagles compiled an overall record of 1–10, with a mark of 0–6 in conference play, and finished seventh in the OVC. Morehead was found to have played an ineligable player, and as a result forefieted four of their victories.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-courier-journal-morehead-to-forfeit/162441157/|work=The Courier-Journal|title=Morehead to forfeit two OVC football wins|date=May 12, 1990|accessdate=January 6, 2025|via=[[Newspapers.com]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.newspapers.com/article/the-courier-journal-middle-tenn-getss-o/162441512/|work=The Courier-Journal|title=Middle Tenn. getss OVC nod|date=August 3, 1990|accessdate=January 6, 2025|via=[[Newspapers.com]]}}</ref>
